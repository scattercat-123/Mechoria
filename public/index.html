ABOUT US

What is mechoria?

Mechoria is a BattleBots-style competition that brings together hardware enthusiasts under the age of eighteen to compete for first place. It is the first student-hosted event of its kind in Bangalore.


It challenges participants to turn concepts into fully working machines and provides an exciting testing ground for young innovators. Mechoria is more than just a competition; it assesses problem-solving, strategic planning, and—above all—engineering proficiency, encouraging the next generation to push the limits of practical technology and design.


What can you do in Mechoria?

Mechoria is a tournamental competitive bot-vs-bot hackathon blending creativity, robotics, and strategic engineering. Here participants can choose to form a team and fight with other teams.

  • Three different weight classes:
    • AntWeight – 1 kg
    • FeatherWeight – 2 kg
    • SkibidiWeight – 3 kg
  • 2–4 week preparation period

    • We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to participate. To support this, we sponsor student projects across all weight classes by providing grants tailored to each category. Teams are required to plan and submit their project proposals for review before funding is approved. Participants may also choose to self-fund their projects if they prefer.
